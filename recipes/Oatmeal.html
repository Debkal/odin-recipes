Cinnamon Apple Oatmeal

Description

This is an easy and delicious take on Oatmeal.

Ingredients

  • 100g Old fashioned oatmeal
  • 2g cinnamon
  • 1 whole medium sized apple (50g to 70g)
  • 15g brown sugar
  • 2g salt
  • 35g water
  • 35g milk(optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a bowl with Oatmeal and water.
  2. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  3. While it is microwaving, dice up the apple into small cubes.
  4. Mix the apple and the brown sugar in a different bowl.
  5. Once it the oats are done microwaving mix in the salt and apples.
  6. Proceed to microwave for 1 more minute.
  7. Add milk after done cooking!
